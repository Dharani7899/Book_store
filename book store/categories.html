Horror Books

Book:Childern To The Slaughter

Author:A.L.Nasser

cost:₹200

SUMMARY:

Children to the Slaughter is a typical small-town horror novella where children are kidnapped by some supernatural beast that runs amuck at night. The story has promise, but I just feel it played its cards way too close for comfort. The prose is so very clerical and plain to the point that it became aggravating.

FEEL GOOD BOOK

Book:The Blue Umberlla

Author:Ruskin Bond

cost:₹600

SUMMARY:

The Blue Umbrella follows the simple story of Binya, a mountain girl, who chances upon a blue umbrella and gets it for herself in exchange for her lucky charm. The entire village becomes envious of the umbrella, including the shopkeeper, Ram Bharosa, who tries to buy the beautiful umbrella from Binya.
